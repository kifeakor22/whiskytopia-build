WHO WE ARE?
Welcome to Whiskytopia - A Scotch Whisky Gifts review site with a difference! Firstly, we tell it like it is with personality & sense of humour. We carefully select Whisky Gift items to review that we personally would either like to receive or give to loved ones or meaningful people in our lives. Always reviewed with passionate & enthusiasm. Curating various types of Whisky Gifts and organising under different occasions / levels of whisky experience. Saving you time and effort! Founded by two lifelong friends and proud Glasweigans who are passionate about their native homeland Scotland and it's national drink - the water of life (Whisky)! Our aim is to showcase, educate, share with you our years of experience of personal enjoyment of various drams merged with our extensive research of some leading online whisky distilleries and retailers. We're glad you stopped by. Stay a while, have some fun, interact with us and the wider Whisky community. Learn and share your own Whisky experiences & journey with us. Remember, "Today's Rain....Tomorrows Whisky!". Whisky industry is opening up, appealing to new generations and audiences. Be a part of the renaissance! Views our the founders own.

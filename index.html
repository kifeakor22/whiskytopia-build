WHO WE ARE?
Welcome to Whiskytopia! We review the best Scotch Whisky Gifts from other carefully selected U.K. retailers. We organise them as suggestions depending on the occasion! Founded by a pair of lifelong pals from Glasgow, Scotland. We’re ultra passionate about Scotland, her lands, people & national drink - Scotch Whisky! Not another stuffy Whisky blog / review site - No c’mon ! We tell it like it is with personality, passion & a sense of humour. We have fun carefully selecting Whisky Gift items to review that we would either like to receive or give to loved ones. Our aim is to showcase, educate, share with you our years of experience of personal enjoyment of various Scotch combined with our extensive research of some leading online whisky distilleries and retailers. We're glad you stopped by. Stay a while, have some fun, interact with us and share your own personal Whisky journey with the wider Whisky community. Remember, "Today's Rain....Tomorrows Whisky!” Views are the founders own. Please note we may be paid commission from online retailers for any products that you may choose to purchase via our website's links. Over 18's Only. Please enjoy responsibly. Sip not slurp! Slàinte Mhath! (Cheers).
